Manufacturing

Inside Our Factory

Raw Material Purification

The process begins with rigorous water purification and raw ingredient sourcing. Every input is filtered and tested to meet international food safety standards before production commences.

Liquid & Steam Processing

Beverage formulations are mixed and processed under controlled steam and temperature conditions — ensuring precise blending, sterilization, and consistency across every production batch.

Rapid Can Formation

High-speed rapid blowing technology forms beverage cans at scale with structural precision. This automated stage ensures dimensional consistency and integrity across the full production volume.

Production & Filling

High-capacity automated filling lines feed and fill cans at production scale. Precision filling technology ensures accurate volume, consistent carbonation, and zero contamination across every unit.

Sealing & Inspection

Each filled can is sealed under controlled conditions and rotated through automated inspection systems. This stage verifies seal integrity, fill level, and structural quality before advancing to packaging.

Final Packaging

Finished products are packaged, labelled, and consolidated for shipment. Final packaging is coordinated to meet each client's export requirements — ready for your market, your shelf, your brand.

Quality & Compliance

Verified Manufacturing Standards

Every product manufactured through our network is backed by internationally recognized quality management systems and certification standards — ensuring full compliance for global distribution across regulated and premium markets.

Our manufacturing infrastructure holds certifications covering food safety management, hazard control, U.S. regulatory compliance, Taiwan national quality standards, and Halal production requirements — providing verified assurance to international buyers and brand partners worldwide.

ISO 22000

International food safety management system standard. Demonstrates that our manufacturing network operates a robust, systematic food safety framework — covering hazard analysis, supply chain traceability, and continuous operational improvement across all production stages.

HACCP

Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points. A globally recognized preventive food safety framework that systematically identifies and controls physical, chemical, and biological hazards at every critical stage of the production process — from raw material to final product.

FDA

U.S. Food and Drug Administration compliance. Our production meets FDA standards for food and beverage manufacturing — a critical requirement for international brands targeting North American retail, distribution, and import channels. Essential for any brand with U.S. market ambitions.

CAS Certified

Chinese Agricultural Standard — Taiwan's national quality certification mark. Confirms that products meet Taiwan's rigorous domestic quality, safety, and agricultural production standards. Provides internationally recognized assurance for buyers sourcing directly from Taiwan manufacturing infrastructure.

HALAL

Certified compliant with Islamic dietary law and production standards. Enables international distribution across Muslim-majority markets including Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Central Asia, and North Africa — significantly expanding the total addressable market for international beverage brands and private label clients.
